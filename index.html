Frequently Asked Questions

How can I download past papers?

Navigate to the Past Papers section, select your stream and subject, then click on the desired paper to download it in PDF format.

When will the 2025 A/L results be released?

The results for the 2025 G.C.E. Advanced Level Examination are expected to be released in March 2026. Exact dates will be announced by the Department of Examinations.

How can I correct my personal information in the exam application?

You need to contact the Department of Examinations directly with supporting documents to correct any personal information in your examination application.
